Tool: OrthoVenn2 [Online]
Perform: Comparison and annotation of orthologous gene clusters among multiple species

Website: https://orthovenn2.bioinfotoolkits.net/home

Usage:
>Open Website >Start >Bacteria
>Upload protein sequences [protein fasta file for each species or view Input Format given]
>Start analysis

Result:
venn diagram, pairwise heatmap, protein clusters, protein count etc
