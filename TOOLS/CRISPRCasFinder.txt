Tool: CRISPRCasFinder
Perform: Detect CRISPR repeats and cas genes from the bacterial genomic sequence

Website: https://crisprcas.i2bc.paris-saclay.fr/

Usage: 
>Go to website >Click on CRISPRCasFinder
>Enter NCBI Accession ID or upload the genome (FASTA format)
>Enter mail ID and job name
>Click on Run CRISPRCasFinder button
