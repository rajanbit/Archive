Tool: VGG Image Annotator (VIA)
Perform: Manual annotation of image for DL based segmentation task

GitHub: https://github.com/nearkyh/via-1.0.5
Website: https://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~vgg/software/via

Installation: 
$ wget http://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~vgg/software/via/downloads/via-src-1.0.4.zip
$ unzip via-src-1.0.4.zip

Usage:
$ firefox via-src-1.0.4/via.html
