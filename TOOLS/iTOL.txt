Tool: Interactive Tree Of Life (iTOL)
Perform: Online tool for phylogenetic tree display and annotation

>Website
https://itol.embl.de/
https://itol.embl.de/upload.cgi

>Usage
Go to @ https://itol.embl.de/upload.cgi
Upload or Paste the tree in Newick, Nexus or PhyloXML format

>Results
The graphical representations of phylogenetic trees will open, here annotation can be done.
